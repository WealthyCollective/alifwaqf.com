Continuous Compliance. Across Every Stage.

The world's first AI-native Shariah compliance scoring and monitoring layer built directly into a venture intelligence platform. Monitor every portfolio company against Shariah principles in real time — from screening through exit — with automated guidance directives issued directly to portfolio companies.

S
Screening Layer
Every inbound deal is pre-screened against Shariah criteria before it enters your pipeline. Revenue sources, business activities, capital structure, and leverage ratios all assessed automatically.
M
Portfolio Monitoring
Post-investment, the platform continuously tracks market signals, financial filings, news, and company-submitted data for compliance drift or emerging concerns.
A
Auto-Issued Directives
When a compliance concern is detected, the platform automatically generates and issues a guidance directive to the portfolio company with specific corrective actions required.
